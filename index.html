About Us

Equatorial Tours was founded to share Rwanda’s unique beauty with the world. Our expert local guides create immersive experiences that connect travelers with nature, wildlife, and authentic culture.

We specialize in gorilla trekking, lake escapes, forest canopy walks, safari adventures, city discovery tours and conservation-led travel. Every journey is crafted to be safe, comfortable and unforgettable.

Our mission: To deliver premium Rwandan experiences while supporting local communities and protecting wildlife.
